About This Server

This webserver is powered by nginx.
It runs on top of a 2006 Mac Mini with a 1.83GHz Intel Core 2 CPU and 1.5GiB of 667MHz DDR2 SDRAM.
It has 80GB of (internal) storage space and an Intel GMA950 media accelerator.
Oh yeah, and it has a floppy disk. Needless to say, it's very weak.
