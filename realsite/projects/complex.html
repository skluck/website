Complex

Status: Complete

Complex is an implementation of complex numbers in Java.
It provides the basic real and imaginary components of a complex number, as well as a set of simple math functions.

Functionality:

  • Cartesian complexes (a + bi notation).
  • Primitive overloaded methods (add, subtract, multiply, divide, pow, etc).
  • Trigonometic methods (sin, cos, tan, sinh, cosh, tanh).
