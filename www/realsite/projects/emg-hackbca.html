EMG (HackBCA)

Status: Hackathoned

Receives data and writes data from an Olimex EMG/EKG shield on an Arduino Uno, using said data in a varity of proof-of-concept programs.
Developed during the HackBCA high schooler hackathon at Bergen County Academy in Hackensack, NJ.
Interfacing is performed via an Arduino Wiring program based upon Olimex samples, while the PoC programs are written from scratch in Processing.
For images from the hackathon, see the picturebook.
